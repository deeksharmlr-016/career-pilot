# **App Name**: PathPilot AI

## Core Features:

- AI Career Fit Scoring Engine: Utilize an AI tool to analyze user skills, experience, and career aspirations, providing a 'Career Fit Score' for target roles and a transparent breakdown of identified skill gaps.
- Dynamic Roadmap Generator: Generate personalized, monthly structured career roadmaps using an AI tool, adapting plans based on user's background, learning speed, and task completion progress.
- Roadmap Management & Progress Tracking: Allow users to view and interact with their generated career roadmaps, mark tasks as complete, and track their overall progress within their execution plans, stored in PostgreSQL.
- Community-Validated Roadmaps: Enable users to browse, rate, and upvote roadmaps shared by the community, fostering collaborative learning and providing social proof for effective career paths.
- AI Resume and Portfolio Builder: Provide an AI tool to assist users in crafting and optimizing their resumes and portfolios, tailoring content to align with target job descriptions and identified skill sets.
- User Authentication & Profile: Secure user registration and login, along with comprehensive profile management to store personal details, career goals, and track progress, leveraging PostgreSQL for data storage.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary brand color: A deep, professional blue (#2E5EAC) to symbolize trust, stability, and intelligence in career guidance.
- Background color: A very light, subtle blue-grey (#F5F7F9) providing a clean, spacious, and focused canvas for complex information.
- Accent color: A vibrant yet clear aqua (#26C4E6) to highlight key actions and signify innovation and clarity on the user's path.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, tech-forward, and bold presentation, reflecting the AI-powered nature of the app.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) chosen for its exceptional readability across various screen sizes, ensuring clear communication of detailed roadmaps and analysis.
- Use modern, clean line icons that clearly communicate actions and concepts related to career progression, learning, AI, and community interaction.
- Implement a clean, organized, and responsive layout, prioritizing easy navigation and clear presentation of complex data like roadmaps and skill analysis on any device.
- Incorporate subtle and purposeful animations for transitions between sections, data updates, and progress indicators to enhance user engagement without distraction.
